Politics in Austria

Parliamentary Democracy

Austria is a parliamentary democracy and a federal republic. The political system is based on the principle of separation of powers.

Learn More

Federal President

The Federal President is the head of state in Austria and is elected directly for a term of six years.

Political Parties

ÖVP

The Austrian People's Party (Österreichische Volkspartei) is a conservative political party in Austria.

SPÖ

The Social Democratic Party of Austria (Sozialdemokratische Partei Österreichs) is a social-democratic political party in Austria. It is one of the major political parties and has a long history in Austrian politics.

FPÖ

The Freedom Party of Austria (Freiheitliche Partei Österreichs) is a right-wing populist and national-conservative political party in Austria.
