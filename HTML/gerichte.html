Austrian National Dishes

Wiener Schnitzel

Wiener Schnitzel is a traditional Austrian dish made from breaded and fried veal cutlets. It is often served with a slice of lemon, parsley potatoes, and lingonberry jam.

Sachertorte

Sachertorte is a famous Austrian chocolate cake with a thin layer of apricot jam, usually served with whipped cream. It is a classic dessert in Austrian cuisine.

Kaiserschmarrn

Kaiserschmarrn is a fluffy shredded pancake dessert served with powdered sugar, applesauce, or plum compote. It is a popular dish in Austria and Bavaria.

Learn More

Tafelspitz

Tafelspitz is a traditional Austrian boiled beef dish, often served with horseradish sauce and a mix of root vegetables. It is considered a classic Sunday family meal.

Learn More

Apfelstrudel

Apfelstrudel is a classic Austrian dessert made with layers of thin pastry dough filled with spiced apple slices, raisins, and breadcrumbs. It is typically served warm with vanilla sauce or whipped cream.
